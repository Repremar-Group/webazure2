REPREMAR Air Cargo / ABOUT US


REPREMAR AIR CARGO,through CIELOSUR, provides, both in Uruguay and Paraguay, 'tailor-made' solutions that meet the needs of both our clients and our customer airlines, and is setting new standards of service in the cargo industry. CIELOSUR (KIXOLER S.A.), was established in 2008 and it is a partnership between AIR CLASS and REPREMAR GROUP. AIR CLASS is an leading regional air cargo carrier with vast activity in South America.
Our clients have recognized our professionalism, expertise and commitment – attributes that have made our company a competitive operation from the outset. Our ability to provide a high-quality service stems from our:
• Attention to detail and dedication to serving our client's best interests. • Knowledge of local market conditions. • Excellent rapport with exporters, freight forwarders and cargo terminal operators. • Professional sales staff with decades of freight related experience. • Flexibility: our philosophy is to maintain a flexible attitude towards our customer's requests with a view to always improving our service levels. • Facilities: our offices in Montevideo (Uruguay) and Asunciòn (Paraguay) are located on-airport.

Part of REPREMAR Group

We are part of a Group of Companies offering ship agency, port operations, international cargo transportation and integral logistics services.
